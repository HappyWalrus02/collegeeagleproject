Our Services

🚗

Garage Floor Coatings

Benefits

  • More durable than plain concrete
  • Easier to clean and maintain
  • Chemical and stain resistance
Not ideal for
  • Large-scale industrial loading without heavy-duty system
  • Projects with active moisture issues (needs mitigation)

Process Overview

Prep (clean & repair) → Prime → Apply epoxy/polyurea → Topcoat & cure.

Expected Lifespan

Typically 5–15 years depending on traffic and maintenance.

🏢

Basement & Commercial / Industrial Coatings

Benefits

  • High-impact durability for heavy use
  • Slip and chemical resistance for safety
  • Low-maintenance finishes for busy sites
Not ideal for
  • Delicate historical floors where removal isn't possible
  • Areas with uncontrolled active water intrusion

Process Overview

Concrete profiling → Moisture mitigation (if needed) → High-build coating system → Quality inspection.

Expected Lifespan

20+ years depending on system, traffic, and environmental conditions.

🌳

Patio & Outdoor Surfaces

Benefits

  • UV-stable finishes to prevent fading
  • Slip-resistant textures for safety
  • Weather-resistant seals to reduce damage
Not ideal for
  • Unstable soil or base that requires heavy structural work
  • Areas with constant standing water

Process Overview

Surface cleaning → Repair & patch → Weather-rated coating application → Seal and finish.

Expected Lifespan

Typically 4–12 years depending on UV exposure and freeze/thaw cycles.
